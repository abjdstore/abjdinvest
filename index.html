abjd.store platform provides all e-commerce solutions without any initial or fixed costs for vendors. A modern, sophisticated multi-vendor platform with an infinite variety of products that offers a comprehensive, enjoyable and secure online shopping experience. Registration is free for all vendors, no fees or commissions are collected before any sale transaction.
A platform that works on all interfaces, PCs, Laptops, iPads & Tablets, and all Mobile Phones, in addition to the presence of a dedicated application on android and IOS.


abjd.store is not just an e-commerce platform, it is a tool that makes it easier for vendors to access new markets, grow their customer base, and increase their sales. The platform provides vendors with the tools and support needed to manage their online stores, uploading all products, managing orders, and processing online payments with secure and multiple payment gateways for everyone, in addition to being linked to Google Maps GPS system for estimating shipping costs by geographical region.
The platform provides direct communication methods between vendors and buyers to facilitate logistics operations or process buyers' inquiries and requests. It also supports both Arabic and English languages.

The platform provides a complete control panel/dashboard for vendors to manage their e-stores, upload their products, determine shipping methods, process orders, update their status, and manage accounts. All this is done independently by the vendor, in addition to the presence of technical support by a specialized team.
